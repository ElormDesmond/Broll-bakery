How to Find Us

Driving Directions:

  1. From Accra Central, head east towards Tema
  2. Take the Teshie exit off the main road
  3. Continue straight until you reach Teshie Tsui Beleo
  4. Turn onto Nii Martey Marley Street
  5. Look for No. 26 - B roll Bakes signboard

Public Transportation:

By Trotro

Take any trotro heading to Teshie and alight at Teshie Tsui Beleo. Walk 3 minutes to Nii Martey Marley Street.

By Taxi/Uber/Bolt

Simply tell the driver "B roll Bakes, Nii Martey Marley Street No 26, Teshie Tsui Beleo" or show them this address.
