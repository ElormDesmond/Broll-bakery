Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Orders placed after 6:00 PM will be delivered the next day.

Do you offer custom cake designs?

Yes! We specialize in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special events. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for custom orders.

How far in advance should I place catering orders?

For catering services, we recommend placing orders at least 1 week in advance. For large events (100+ guests), please contact us 2-3 weeks ahead.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian samosas, spring rolls, pies, and all our cakes are vegetarian-friendly. Please specify dietary requirements when ordering.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified up to 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time. Please call us immediately if you need to make changes.
